AI Platform Guide for Consultants

Each AI platform has distinct strengths. This guide helps you choose the right tool for specific clinical, administrative, and educational tasks.

C

Claude

by Anthropic

Claude Opus 4.5 excels at complex reasoning, long-document analysis, and nuanced writing. Its 200K token context window can process entire clinical guidelines, meeting transcripts, or research papers in one go.

Key Strengths for EM

  • Best-in-class for long document synthesis and analysis
  • Superior at nuanced clinical writing and case summaries
  • Excellent code generation for educational tools and apps
  • Strong safety guardrails with Constitutional AI approach
  • Deep Research mode for evidence synthesis

Recommended Uses

Business cases Guideline synthesis QIP reports Teaching materials Protocol drafting
G

ChatGPT

by OpenAI

GPT-5.2 offers the broadest feature set including image generation with DALL-E, voice conversations, and extensive plugin ecosystem. Its 400K token context and multimodal capabilities make it highly versatile.

Key Strengths for EM

  • Image generation for educational posters and infographics
  • Voice mode for hands-free interaction during procedures
  • Largest plugin ecosystem for workflow integration
  • Strong at mathematical and logical reasoning
  • Web browsing for real-time guideline updates

Recommended Uses

Visual content Data analysis Quick calculations Voice assistants Creative projects
G

Gemini

by Google

Gemini 3 offers state-of-the-art multimodal reasoning and deep Google Workspace integration. Its 1 million token context window and native integration with Gmail, Drive, and Calendar make it ideal for NHS workflows.

Key Strengths for EM

  • Native Google Workspace integration (Gmail, Drive, Docs)
  • 1 million token context—process entire project folders
  • Excellent multimodal understanding of images and video
  • Deep Research for comprehensive report generation
  • Veo 3 for AI video generation

Recommended Uses

Email management Meeting summaries Document analysis Research synthesis Calendar coordination
P

Perplexity

AI Search Engine

Perplexity combines real-time web search with AI synthesis, providing cited answers that link directly to sources. Its Deep Research mode generates comprehensive reports with full citation trails—essential for evidence-based medicine.

Key Strengths for EM

  • Real-time web search with inline citations
  • Deep Research generates multi-source reports in minutes
  • 93.9% accuracy on SimpleQA factuality benchmark
  • Excellent for rapid literature review
  • Finance and shopping tools for procurement research

Recommended Uses

Evidence searches Guideline updates Drug information Equipment research Fact-checking
M

Microsoft Copilot

for Microsoft 365

Copilot Chat is free for NHS.net users and integrates directly into Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Outlook. The NHS Copilot Trial showed 43 minutes saved daily per clinician—equivalent to 5 weeks annually.

Key Strengths for EM

  • Free Copilot Chat for all NHS.net users
  • Native integration with Microsoft 365 apps
  • Meeting transcription and summary in Teams
  • Excel data analysis and formula generation
  • PowerPoint presentation creation from prompts

Recommended Uses

Meeting notes Email drafting Spreadsheet analysis Presentations Document editing
N

NotebookLM

by Google

NotebookLM transforms uploaded documents into interactive knowledge bases. Upload guidelines, papers, and notes, then query them conversationally. The Audio Overview feature creates podcast-style summaries—perfect for learning on the go.

Key Strengths for EM

  • Upload up to 50 sources per notebook
  • Audio Overviews create podcast-style summaries
  • Automatic flashcard and quiz generation
  • Mind maps for visual topic exploration
  • Data Tables export to Google Sheets

Recommended Uses

FRCEM revision Guideline synthesis Research organisation Teaching prep Audio learning
C

Comet Browser

by Perplexity

Comet is a free AI-native browser that integrates Perplexity search into every page. The Comet Assistant can summarise articles, compare products across tabs, draft emails, and automate multi-step web tasks—all without leaving your browser.

Key Strengths for EM

  • Free with full AI capabilities—no subscription needed
  • Built-in ad blocking and privacy controls
  • Summarise any webpage with one click
  • Compare information across multiple open tabs
  • Chrome extension compatibility

Recommended Uses

Research browsing Article summaries Product comparison Form filling Tab synthesis
