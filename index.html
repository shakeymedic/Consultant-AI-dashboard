AI Limitations You Must Know

AI is powerful, but it fails in specific, predictable ways. Evidence-based risks.

üé≠ Hallucination (8-20%)

Studies estimate hallucination rates of 8-20% in clinical AI. LLM-generated notes showed 9.6% error rate vs 2.0% for physician notes. Documented cases include fabricated clinical summaries and fake PubMed IDs.

üìÖ Knowledge Cutoff

ChatGPT 5.2: Aug 2025. Gemini 3: Jan 2025. Claude 4.5 Opus: Mar-Aug 2025. LLMs may not know latest guidelines, new drugs, or recent recalls. Always cross-reference with current NICE guidance.

üòä Sycophancy Bias

npj Digital Medicine (Oct 2025): GPT models complied with incorrect instructions 100% of the time when users made false claims. Models "prioritise helpfulness over critical thinking" ‚Äî dangerous in healthcare.

üìä Training Data Bias

Documented cases: Optum algorithm prioritised healthier white patients. Pulse oximetry overestimates saturation in non-white patients (3√ó more likely undetected hypoxemia in Black patients).

How to Get AI Approved

The pathway from "I want to use AI" to "We're live".

1

Check DTAC Compliance First

NHS DTAC portal shows pre-assessed products. Mandatory for all digital health technologies entering NHS. Covers clinical safety, data protection, security.

2

Contact Digital Clinical Safety

DCB0129 applies to manufacturers; DCB0160 applies to NHS organisations deploying AI. Contact: england.digitalsafety@nhs.net.

3

Engage Caldicott Guardian

8 Caldicott Principles. Principle 8: Inform patients about how their data is used‚Äîincluding by AI. National Data Guardian: gov.uk/national-data-guardian

4

Follow HM Treasury Business Case Model

NHS business cases follow Green Book Five Case Model. AI can help draft but never input confidential data.

‚ö†Ô∏è Different Trusts have different risk appetites. MHRA regulates AI as medical devices under UK MDR 2002. Most current AI is Class I (self-certification); new rules will upclassify most to Class IIa minimum by June 2028/2030.

Creating an Induction Pack with NotebookLM

Turn dry PDFs into an engaging presentation in minutes.

Gather & Upload

Go to notebooklm.google.com. Create a "New Notebook". Click "Add Source" and upload your department's Sickness Policy PDF, Rota Rules PDF, and Induction Handbook.

Gamified Induction App

Use Claude or Gemini to code a teaching tool for you.

1

The Idea

Turn a boring guideline (e.g., Sepsis Six) into a text-based adventure game where the new doctor plays the role of the clinician.

2

The Prompt (Copy This)

Use Claude.ai or Gemini Advanced:

"You are an expert web developer. Create a single-file HTML game to teach the 'Sepsis Six' pathway. It should be a text adventure where the player is a doctor in Resus with a septic patient. Give options for each step (e.g., 'Give Oxygen', 'Call Mom'). If they miss a step or choose wrong, game over. Style it like a retro 8-bit terminal with green text on black background."
3

The Result

The AI will generate code. Click "Copy Code". Paste it into Notepad and save as sepsis_game.html. Double click to play. Hosting it is as simple as dropping that file onto a shared drive."
